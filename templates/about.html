About Lapla

Lapla is a cutting-edge web platform that delivers professional-grade Formula 1 telemetry analysis with real-time data visualization, advanced circuit mapping, and AI-powered performance insights for racing enthusiasts, engineers, and data scientists worldwide.

Our Mission

To democratize access to F1 data analysis tools and make the fascinating world of Formula 1 telemetry accessible to everyone - from casual fans to professional engineers.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for F1 data analysis, helping users understand the intricate details that make the difference between victory and defeat in Formula 1.

Key Features

Advanced Telemetry

6-chart telemetry suite: Speed profiles, throttle/brake analysis, gear strategies, RPM data, DRS zones, and circuit layouts with speed mapping.

Driver Comparison

Multi-driver analysis with sector timing breakdowns, fuel efficiency ratings, and performance metrics comparison.

AI-Powered Insights

OpenAI-powered performance analysis with strategic recommendations and key findings for selected drivers.

Sprint Support

Complete support for Sprint weekends including Sprint Qualifying and Sprint race data analysis.

Circuit Visualization

Real-time circuit generation with speed-colored track segments using plasma colormap visualization.

Data Export

Export telemetry analysis in JSON and CSV formats for further research and analysis.

Weather Integration

Live weather conditions display with air/track temperature, humidity, wind, and pressure data.

Interactive Charts

Zoom, fullscreen, and enlargement capabilities for detailed telemetry data examination.
